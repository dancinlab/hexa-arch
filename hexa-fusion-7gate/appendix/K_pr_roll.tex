\section{Pull-Request Roll}
\label{app:pr-roll}

This appendix is the verbatim transcription of the
\texttt{companion/\allowbreak pr-roll.json} session pull-request roll.
It will be rendered as a longtable, one row per PR, carrying every
field from the JSON record:
the \texttt{repo} (hexa-lang stdlib SSOT vs.\ demiurge paper home), the
PR \texttt{number}, the \texttt{title}, the \texttt{merged\_at}
timestamp, the \texttt{gh}-measured diff-stat (\texttt{files\_changed},
\texttt{lines\_added}, \texttt{lines\_deleted}), the \texttt{gate} it
serves, and its \texttt{role} in the dependency chain.

The PRs are presented in dependency order, matching the
Reproducibility section's landing list (\S\ref{sec:repro}): the
hexa-lang stdlib PRs (\#1012 \ldots \#1102) followed by the demiurge
paper-home PRs. Data source: \texttt{companion/pr-roll.json}.
