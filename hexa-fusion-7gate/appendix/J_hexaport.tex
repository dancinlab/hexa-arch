\section{HEXA-PORT Sibling --- Native Solver Port Roadmap}
\label{app:hexaport}

This appendix is the full roadmap for HEXA-PORT, the sibling project
that reimplements the external solvers natively in hexa-lang
(\S\ref{sec:context}). It will expand the tiered ladder: \textbf{Tier
A} (closed-form, already native), \textbf{Tier B} (algorithm-defined
PDE/FEM --- FreeGS and GetDP), and \textbf{Tier C} (data-bound ---
OpenMC, where the $\sim$GB ENDF/B-VIII library is the irreducible
part), mapped onto the P0--P4 milestone sequence.

The methodological core is the \emph{parity-oracle} pattern: each
wrap-and-fix result in this paper doubles as a numerical oracle that a
future native port must reproduce. The appendix will detail the
Tier-C decomposition (P4a native MC engine, P4b cross-section corpus
port via git-LFS infra from PR \#1090, P4c first-principles
cross-section generation) and state honestly that not all of HEXA-PORT
is in scope for this paper. Data source: \S\ref{sec:context} and the
roadmap notes.
