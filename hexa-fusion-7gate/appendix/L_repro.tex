\section{Reproducibility Runbook}
\label{app:repro}

This appendix is the complete reproducibility runbook for the paper.
It will carry, verbatim, every \texttt{hexa verify --expr <fn> <args>
<expected>} invocation that produces an atom in the verification
ledger (Appendix~\ref{app:verify-ledger}), so a reader can re-run any
single claim in one CLI line. Each invocation will be paired with its
expected verdict block.

The runbook will also pin the external-backend environment
specifications --- \textsc{FreeGS}\,0.8.2 (numpy $<\!2.0$),
\textsc{OpenMC}\,0.15.3 with ENDF/B-VIII.0, and GetDP\,3.5.0/4.0.0 ---
and the canonical data paths: the stdlib kernel home, the GetDP
\texttt{.pro}/\texttt{.geo} decks under
\texttt{stdlib/\allowbreak rtsc/\allowbreak decks/},
the deferred ENDF/B-VIII corpus under \texttt{data/nuclear/endf\_b8\_0/}
(git-LFS infra from PR \#1090), and the \texttt{companion/} data-record
axis. Build/lint targets (\texttt{make}, \texttt{make lint},
\texttt{make pages}) are documented from the Makefile. Data source:
\S\ref{sec:repro} and \texttt{companion/README.md}.
