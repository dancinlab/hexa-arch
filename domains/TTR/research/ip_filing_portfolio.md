## §2 IP-1: HA dissolving MN array + cascade payload (foundational)

### Claims (provisional draft)

```
   Claim 1: A dissolving microneedle (MN) array comprising:
       (a) HA matrix backbone (MW 100-500 kDa);
       (b) needle length 800-1800 μm; tip diameter 5-15 μm; base 100-200 μm;
       (c) needle density 200-2000 per cm²;
       (d) payload comprising at least one enzymatic agent (AzoR, DyP, laccase, peroxidase),
           at least one chelating agent (DFO, DTPA, EDTA, siderophore),
           at least one nanozyme (CeO₂, Cu_xFe₃₋ₓO₄, PrOx, MnO₂),
       (e) for application to tattooed skin to facilitate ink degradation;
       wherein the payload is configured to release upon HA dissolution within 5-30 min.
   
   Claim 2: The MN array of claim 1, wherein the cascade comprises AzoR + DyP + CeO₂.
   Claim 3: ... (density variants, optical add-ons, etc)
   ...
```

## §3 IP-7: Engineered AzoR/DyP variant (★★★★ strongest)

### Claims (composition of matter · strongest IP)

```
   Claim 1: An engineered azoreductase variant having:
       (a) ≥ 50% sequence identity to wild-type AzoR (UniProt P41407);
       (b) at least one mutation selected from:
           - V64A, V64M, V64I (active site widening)
           - T101S (FMN binding affinity)
           - L150P (helix flexibility)
           - W139F (substrate binding)
           [list specific mutations from yeast display rounds]
       (c) K_cat ≥ 500 s⁻¹ on methyl orange substrate (vs WT ~100 s⁻¹);
       (d) for use in tattoo ink degradation;
       wherein the variant has thermal stability T_m ≥ 50°C.
   
   Claim 2: A pharmaceutical composition comprising the engineered variant of claim 1.
   Claim 3: A method of removing tattoo ink comprising administering the variant.
```

## §4 IP-10: AAV6 + AzoR/DyP cassette (★★★★ paradigm IP)

### Claims (gene therapy)

```
   Claim 1: An AAV vector for tattoo ink degradation comprising:
       (a) AAV6, AAV-DJ, or skin-tropic engineered AAV capsid;
       (b) a gene cassette encoding:
           - azoreductase (AzoR · P41407 sequence or 80%+ identical) AND
           - dye-decolorizing peroxidase (DyP type · 80%+ identical)
       (c) flanked by inverted terminal repeats (ITRs);
       (d) optionally:
           - CMV constitutive promoter OR
           - K14 keratinocyte-specific promoter OR
           - inducible promoter (TetON, etc.)
       (e) for intradermal injection at tattooed area.
   
   Claim 2: The vector of claim 1 further comprising a TetON-regulated AzoR expression.
   Claim 3: A method of removing tattoo ink comprising injecting the vector.
   Claim 4: A pharmaceutical preparation comprising the vector at 10⁹-10¹² vg/cm².
```

### Strength

- Paradigm-shift moat (단일 시술 vs MN cycle)
- AAV6 + specific cassette = composition of matter
- Use patent (tattoo indication)
- Defensible against alternative AAV variants

### Filing strategy
