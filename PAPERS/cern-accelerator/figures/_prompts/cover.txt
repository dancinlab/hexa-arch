accelerator, rendered in a precise scientific-diagram style on a dark
navy background with cyan and amber accents. Left to right: (1) an intense
red drive laser pulse enters from the left; (2) it crosses a glowing
turquoise gas-jet plasma region; (3) behind the laser a bright crescent
"wakefield bubble" cavity forms in the plasma, drawn as a luminous
parabolic wave structure; (4) a tightly focused electron bunch (a small
bright dot trailing the bubble) is accelerated forward; (5) the electron
bunch then passes through a row of blue quadrupole magnets arranged as a
FODO lattice (alternating focusing/defocusing, shown as paired magnet
yokes); (6) on the far right, a stylized particle detector (concentric
rings) registers the beam. Subtle field lines and a faint gradient arrow
labeled GV/m above the plasma stage. Minimal, elegant, high-contrast,
no text labels, 16:9 landscape, suitable as the cover figure of a physics
monograph.
