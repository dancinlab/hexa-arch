\begin{abstract}
We present \textbf{UFO}, the \emph{capstone} (integration) volume of the
demiurge stack: a single-seat vehicle that ties together four
independently-verified sibling domains --- \textbf{RTSC}
(real-temperature superconducting magnets), \textbf{FUSION}
(magnetic-confinement / MHD propulsion), \textbf{ANTIMATTER}
($\gamma$-rocket annihilation propulsion), and \textbf{CERN}
(accelerator field / particle infrastructure) --- into one carrier and
carries it through the full demiurge seven-verb pipeline
(\texttt{spec}$\to$\texttt{structure}$\to$\texttt{design}$\to$\texttt{analyze}\,$\circlearrowleft$$\to$\texttt{synthesize}$\to$\texttt{verify}$\to$\texttt{handoff}),
non-wet-lab, end to end. The physical spine is a \textbf{seven-stage
propulsion ladder} (\S\ref{sec:pipeline}): Stage~1 hover (Meissner
levitation on an RTSC \SI{48}{\tesla} solenoid), Stage~2 cruise
(MHD$+$fusion body-force thrust), Stage~3 orbital ($\gamma$-rocket
annihilation exhaust), then Stage~4 warp (Alcubierre), Stage~5 wormhole
(Morris--Thorne), Stage~6 dim-jump (Kaluza--Klein ladder), and Stage~7
dim-use (a composite $\tau\!=\!4$ cycle). The ladder carries a
deliberately \emph{sharp honesty wall}: Stages~1--3 are closed-form
verified (\tierGreen, inherited verbatim from the sibling domains ---
\texttt{ioffe\_loop\_bz}, \texttt{triple\_product},
\texttt{pair\_threshold\_total}$+$\texttt{rel\_kinetic\_from\_p}, all
\textsc{Supported-Numerical} at $|\Delta|\!=\!0$, atlas hash
\texttt{663698a0}), while Stages~4--7 are a \emph{13-falsifier
preregister, ALL OPEN} (\tierWhite, academically \textbf{UNPROVEN}).
We do \emph{not} claim warp, wormhole, dimensional jump, or dimensional
use is achieved; we register, for each, the precise measurement that
would falsify it. The capstone's headline verdict is honest:
\textbf{\texttt{absorbed=false}}. Five non-wet-lab digital-twin gates
(CFD, EM 3D, structural FEA, the $\circlearrowleft$ fixed-point
coupling, and one cross-coupled gate) have not converged to a body-solve
(they are deferred to pool/cloud heavy simulation), and Stages~4--7 are
all open --- so the integration bit cannot flip. A design that clears
all non-wet-lab gates is a \emph{witness of integrated-vehicle
feasibility}, never a built or flown craft. Across the ladder we
observe a shared lattice constant $B^{*}=\sigma\!\cdot\!\tau=48$ that
recurs in RTSC ($H_{c2}$), FUSION (Lawson target), and the UFO Meissner
lift --- but this is an \emph{arithmetic-reuse observation}, not a proof
of integrated physics. Bit-for-bit reproduction artifacts (verify
ledger, PR roll, integration map, session journal) ship under
\texttt{companion/}.
\end{abstract}

\section{Introduction}
\label{sec:intro}

The four sibling domains of the demiurge stack each answer a single,
physically distinct propulsion question to a verified closed form: RTSC
asks how strong a real-temperature superconducting magnet can be (and
how much lift its Meissner fringe field provides); FUSION asks whether a
magnetically-confined plasma can both ignite and exert an MHD body-force
thrust; ANTIMATTER asks what specific impulse an annihilation
$\gamma$-rocket can reach; and CERN asks how accelerator-grade fields
and relativistic-kinematics infrastructure are produced and verified.
Each domain has its own monograph and its own verify ledger. None of
them, on its own, answers the \emph{integration} question: \emph{can a
single carrier consume all four, in sequence, as the rungs of one
propulsion ladder?}

\textbf{UFO is the capstone that asks exactly that.} It is not a fifth
physics domain --- it adds essentially no new physics atoms. It is an
\emph{integration volume}: it consumes the four siblings' verified
records, places them on a seven-stage propulsion ladder, and runs the
whole vehicle through the demiurge seven-verb design pipeline
(\texttt{spec}$\to$\texttt{handoff}) to a non-wet-lab completed form
(\S\ref{sec:pipeline7}). The contribution is therefore architectural
honesty under integration pressure: keeping a sharp wall between the
three rungs that are genuinely closed-form-verified and the four rungs
that are open, unproven speculation --- even as they sit in one table,
on one vehicle, under one title.

\section{Background --- the Capstone Thesis and the Verify Tier Rubric}
\label{sec:bg}

\paragraph{The integration thesis.} A magnetic-levitation magnet, a
fusion-MHD thruster, and an antimatter $\gamma$-rocket each clear a
different physical regime: hover near the ground, cruise through the
atmosphere, and accelerate to orbital/escape velocity. The capstone
thesis is that these are not three separate vehicles but three
\emph{rungs} of one ladder, mounted on one disc-shaped single-seat
carrier, switched between by closed-form altitude/velocity/$\Delta v$
triggers. Stages~4--7 are notionally the upper rungs of the same ladder
(interstellar and beyond), but --- and this is the load-bearing honesty
--- they require negative-energy exotic matter, unobserved extra
dimensions, and synthetic closure loops that are academically
\textbf{unproven}. The capstone's whole job is to integrate the three
proven rungs honestly while preregistering the four open ones.

\paragraph{Why a capstone, not a fifth domain.} UFO adds no new physics
kernels: every numerical atom it cites is \emph{inherited} from a
sibling domain's verify ledger and is already folded into the hexa-lang
atlas SSOT (\texttt{compiler/atlas/embedded.gen.hexa}, hash
\texttt{663698a0}). The capstone's deliverable is the \emph{integration}
--- the seven-verb design package (BOM, drawings, firmware architecture,
digital-twin plan) for a single vehicle that consumes all four siblings
--- not new science. This is what keeps the work honest: the proven
content is exactly the siblings' proven content, and nothing is upgraded
in tier by the act of integration.

\paragraph{The verify tier rubric.} Each quantity is assigned a tier
under the \texttt{hexa verify} rubric: \tierBlue\ \textsc{Supported-Formal}
(a closed-form / proof-level integer identity reproduced exactly),
\tierGreen\ \textsc{Supported-Numerical} (a reproducible libm-class
recompute matching a persisted reference within $\varepsilon=10^{-9}$),
\tierYellow\ \textsc{Supported-By-Citation} (a composite physics formula
backed by a literature DOI), \tierOrange\
\textsc{Insufficient/Deferred} (needs a heavyweight body-solve or
wet-lab measurement), \tierRed\ \textsc{Falsified} (a controlled
negative, retained as honest evidence), and \tierWhite\
\textsc{Speculation-Fenced} (an open, academically unproven claim with a
preregistered falsifier). The rubric is the contract that lets a reader
audit any claim without re-running it, and it is the spine of the
pipeline table that follows. \textbf{Crucially, \tierWhite\ never
graduates to \tierGreen\ by integration} --- only an empirical
observation can move a Stage-4--7 falsifier from \texttt{OPEN} to
\texttt{CONFIRMED} or \texttt{DEMOTED}.

genuine closed-form recomputes, each with a \tierGreen\
\textsc{Supported-Numerical} atom at $|\Delta|=0$:
\begin{itemize}\itemsep0.2em
\item \textbf{Stage 1 hover} --- the hover-coil on-axis field is
  \texttt{ioffe\_loop\_bz}$(a,I,\zeta\!=\!0)=\mu_0 I/(2a)$; for a
  \SI{90}{\kilogram} pilot the Meissner lift
  $F_{\text{lev}}=1\!\cdot\!0.05\!\cdot\!2\!\cdot\!20/\mu_0
  \approx\SI{1.59e6}{\newton}$ is $\sim$1800$\times$ the
  \SI{882.9}{\newton} weight (closed-form ample margin).

  is $F_{\text{MHD}}=J\!\cdot\!B\!\cdot\!V_{\text{ch}}

  verified through the \texttt{triple\_product} atom; two diagonal

  relativistic exhaust whose photon ceiling is
  $I_{\text{sp,max}}=c/g\approx\SI{3.06e7}{\second}$, anchored by

\end{itemize}
These three are \emph{inherited}, not re-derived: each atom is already
folded into the atlas SSOT by its parent domain, and UFO's reuse is an

\paragraph{The falsifier ladder (Stages 4--7).} The upper four rungs do

claimed achieved.} The lattice integers they reference

traversable throats, and $100c$ composite speeds the integers point to
are not.
